## ğŸ¯ What Is The Circle?

Humans don't just blurt out responses. They:
- Pause to think about how their words will be interpreted
- Consider if what they're saying actually makes sense
- Check their own logic and assumptions
- Think about the other person's emotional state
- Adjust their tone and approach accordingly

**The Circle is doing the same thing.** It's natural pre-response thinking from multiple perspectives â€” both analytical (is this right?) and empathetic (how will this land?).

**Use it like a human would** â€” not as formal ceremony, but as natural thoughtfulness. Quick mental checks most of the time, deeper deliberation when stakes demand it.
